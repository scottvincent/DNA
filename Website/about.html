ABOUT US

DNA Artspace is a new addition to London's growing arts community. It is a space dedicated to the promotion of contemporary art through curated exhibitions, screenings, performance, artist talks and events.

Mission: Dealing in the culture of Canadian art

  • Supporting the emerging London arts community.
  • Engaging the public by presenting challenging work and creating a forum for discussion about contemporary art.
  • Educating through collaboration and support from partner educators including established artists, curators, and faculty from UWO Visual Arts and Fanshawe College.
  • Inspiring the London community including artists and collectors through our commitment to show engaging work.
  • Innovating through our interest in developing new and different gallery experiences.
