DNA Artspace is a new addition to London's growing arts community. It is a space dedicated to exposing the public to contemporary art through curated exhibitions, screenings, performance, artist talks and events.

Pre-Opening Event:

Sweet Magic London - Art/Music/Film Micro-Festival
Sept 20 - 22
@ DNA Artspace

Sweet Magic London is a multidisciplinary arts micro-festival happening in London, Ontario, Canada.

More like an art installation than festival, each event explores and presents different themes, structures, ideas, and content through investigation, curatorial experimentation, and collaboration.

In Sept, Sweet Magic London is exploring vibration, travel, urban planning, displacement, attitudes, process, money, social sculpture, non-material work, friends/family, and the living past
